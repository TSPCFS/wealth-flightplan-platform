R5k/month for 25 years

WE-3 · Step 6: Investment

Foundation Momentum Freedom
Key principle
Magic of consistent monthly saving + time horizon.

The headline example. R5,000/month invested from age 35 to 60 compounding monthly at 10% nominal grows to ~R6.6m. At the 4% safe-withdrawal rate that supplies R22.1k/month of passive income, indefinitely.

WE-3 anchors the entire investment chapter. Adjust monthly contribution or years, and notice how years moves the outcome dramatically more than contribution. That's the lever WE-4 and WE-5 push on next.
